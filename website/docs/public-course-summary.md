# Public Course Summary

This workshop trains researchers to use AI agents safely and productively in economics and business research.

## Who this is for

- PhD students and research assistants
- Applied economists and business researchers
- Instructors designing agent-enabled teaching

## What participants can do after two days

- Design prompts with clear constraints and checks
- Use coding agents for focused bugfix/refactor tasks
- Build a small agent workflow with tool and memory contracts
- Evaluate outputs with reliability and risk criteria

## Course shape

- Day 1: foundations, prompting, coding agents, risk basics
- Day 2: orchestration, research pipeline integration, evaluation, resilience
